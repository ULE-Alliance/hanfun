6 __Q1 : I have updated my HAN-FUN library and now my application callbacks are not being called ?__
7

9 a _virtual_ function is being overloaded.

12 warning if the method signature you are using has been deprecated.

29 __Q2 : Can the HAN-FUN library be linked into a shared library ?__
30 
31 Yes, however you must pass the __HF_SHARED_SUPPORT__ option when configuring the library.
32 This option will remove the flag '-fno-rtti' and add the '-fPIC' flag from the compilations flags.
33 
34 The building instructions become :
35 
36  $ mkdir build/
37  $ cd build/
38  $ cmake -DHF_SHARED_SUPPORT=ON ..
39  $ make
